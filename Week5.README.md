# Cardano Study Session Report 
**Date:** Thursday, 27rd November 2025  
**Time:** 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM  
**Venue:** Blockchain Centre NBO, Maralal Oasis, 4th Floor, Room 401


## Overview
The Cardano study session covered a range of activities focused on community engagement, learning, and hands-on development. The session encouraged participation, practical learning, and networking among members.


## Activities

### 1. Pixel Concept Introduction
This concept, introduced by **Darlington**, illustrated how Cardano meetups can **visually express decentralization and community involvement**. Each pixel represented a supporter or project, creating a **collective display of the ecosystem**. The activity encouraged:
- Participation
- Small contributions
- Creative engagement during events



### 2. CBCA Course Onboarding
Participants were **onboarded onto the CBCA course**, with engagement incentivized by **awarding the top student on the leaderboard**. This activity:
- Encouraged active participation
- Motivated learners to complete the course
- Introduced a friendly competitive element to enhance learning outcomes



### 3. Assignment Presentation
Participants presented the **assignment from the previous session**, sharing:
- Observations
- Stories
- Documentation gathered  
This demonstrated their understanding of the **community as a living system**.



### 4. Andamio Site Onboarding & Building on Cardano Module
New members were onboarded to the **Andamio site** and enrolled in the **Building on Cardano** module. Activities included a  overview of :
- Setting up a **Cardano wallet**
- Preparing the **development environment**
- Starting a **MeshJS project**
- Connecting the wallet and using **wallet hooks**

The assignment **“My First Cardano Wallet Interface”** guided participants to:
- Build a simple wallet interface demonstrating core concepts
- Optionally perform **on-chain transactions**



### 5. Networking Session
The session concluded with a **networking session** featuring **food and drinks**, allowing participants to connect, discuss ideas, and share experiences in an informal setting.
