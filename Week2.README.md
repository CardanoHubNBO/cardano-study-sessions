### Study Sessions, Networking over Refreshments and Food (7:10 PM – 8:30 PM)

The final portion of the evening provided a relaxed and vibrant atmosphere for attendees to network while enjoying dinner and refreshments. This was a valuable opportunity for participants to discuss what they had learned, share ideas, and strengthen connections within the Cardano community. Conversations ranged from the future of blockchain technology to personal experiences with Cardano’s ecosystem, further reinforcing the sense of belonging and shared purpose among participants.
